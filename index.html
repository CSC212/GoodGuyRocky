Good Guy Rocky

In this application, NAO is given a paper plate and a fork. Nao uses voice recognition to obtain the correct item and then proceeds to put it down on a table, before turning around and asking for the next object.


Goal: The robot should use voice recognition to obtain the correct object and place it on the table. It should then walk back to take the second item.
